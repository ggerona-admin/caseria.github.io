Bicol Guerilla Museum

Caseria De Fundado, Brgy. Fundado, Canaman, Camarines Sur, Fundado, Philippines, 4402

The Bicol Guerilla Museum honors the bravery of Bicolano fighters during WWII through rare artifacts, photos, and memorabilia. It preserves the region’s legacy of resistance and educate visitors on the local fight for freedom and national pride.
